Am I going to get a mock up?

Yes. The design of your site is very important and as the owner, you will be involved in the design process with us.

How to keep track of progress

There will be regular communication on how things are going.

You may also have a preview version of the site where you can see real time progress and changes.

What will you need from me?

You the client should provide an overview of what your site is for (branding? selling products? selling services?) so that we the developers know what to build.

Having some content for your site is also encouraged. Content in the form of text, images etc is great as copywriting and graphic design are entire disciplines by themselves.

Anything else will be discussed in the planning stage of your website.

Can you write the text for me?

Yes, we can. Copywriting is a skill we have available in-house and in uncertain events, can outsource to third parties.

Will you get images for me?

General pictures from the internet will be sourced for you. Naturally too, specific pictures about your product or service would be provided by you.

How do you build my website?

There are quite a variety of technologies out there. If you are tech savy and have a personal set of technologies you would like to use for your website, we are open to discussion.

All in all, we are very open about what particular tech we will be using to build your site. The main aim is to build your site in the best way possible.

Who owns the website once it's paid for?

In short, it is your website, But I have two words for you, Legal Contract. This is a vital stage of our web development process and it clearly spells out exactly what is, and what is not.

A Legal Contract does not prove a lack of trust but rather, it shows that both parties are genuie professionals that seek legitimate business.

Read on the benefits of a Contract.
Are your designs premade templates?

No. We build your site for you. If you have a site whoose design you admire, feel free to draw our attention to it and we would take that inspiration and add a touch of you to it.

Will my website be mobile friendly?

Of course, your site will be responsive to all devices. It would be a disaster if your site performs poorly on mobile devices or breaks on larger screens.

How do we begin work on my project?

We start by getting in touch - whether it's a call from you, an email (or we reach out to you before you reach out to us :) . During our initial meeting, we'll exchange greetings and delve into the specific services you're looking for.

After that, we'll generally schedule a video call, using visual elements to gain a deeper understanding of your project. This allows us to discuss crucial details such as the project's scope, depth, timeline, and pricing, ensuring a comprehensive and successful partnership.

What communication channels do we use?

We prioritize high availability and transparency in our collaboration. Our primary communication methods include

  • Emails
  • Instant Messaging
  • Phone Calls
  • Video Calls
  • Social Media Platforms (If Applicable)

We use the most convinent method for smooth communication.

Will we sign a formal Contract?

In most cases, Yes. A contract serves as a formal agreement that outlines the expectations and responsibilities of both you, the client and us, the web agency. It specifies the scope of work, deliverables, timelines, and payment terms.

A well-drafted contract provides legal protection for both of us. It establishes a clear framework for our project, helping to prevent disputes and misunderstandings.

Read more on why a formal contract is essential.
